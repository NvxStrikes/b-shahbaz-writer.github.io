Meet the Author

Stories that
live in the dark

Bisma Shahbaz is an aspiring author from Pakistan writing gothic romance, literary fiction, and cozy fantasy — the stories she spent years searching for and couldn't find. She believes in the quiet power of Pakistani voices in English literature, and in the kind of fiction that makes you feel less alone at 2AM.

Currently working on several WIPs, writing Substack essays, and looking for beta readers brave enough to venture into her worlds.
